HOW TO COMPRESS IMAGES

Our advanced image compression tool makes it effortless to optimize your images for web and mobile. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking "browse files"
  2. Adjust compression level using the intuitive slider (higher values mean smaller files)
  3. Select output format (keep original or convert to JPG, PNG, or WebP)
  4. Optionally resize your image by enabling the resize option
  5. Click "Compress Image" to process your file with our ultra algorithm
  6. Download your optimized image and enjoy faster loading times
